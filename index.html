Our Story

Three Generations of Gemological Excellence

Founded in Geneva in 1987, Lumière Gems has spent nearly four decades building relationships with the world's most trusted mines, cutters, and gemological laboratories. Every stone in our collection arrives with independent certification and an unbroken provenance chain.

We believe a precious gem is not merely an asset — it is a story, crystallised over millions of years, waiting to be passed down.
